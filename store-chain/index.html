What can QuicShop provide to Store Chains?

80% of all grocery sales happen in the store, so how do stores reach their shoppers while they're between the aisles? QuicShop is the intermediary between the shoppers and the store's online presence.

How it works

Integrating QuicShop with your Store Chain

QuicShop reaches shoppers while they're in the store, promoting multiple ads on our platform during your customer's shopping trip. We also track behavioral data, giving stores insight into how they can maximize store layout to increase profit.

Increase Basket Size

Through the promotion of relevant in-store details we influence impulse buys: Promoting deals that are only steps away, leading to shoppers adding more items to their trip.

Behavioral Data

QuicShop also tracks behavioral data in the form of consumer and search bar analytics. Illustrating how shoppers search and interact with the items in the store.

Benefits

How Store Chains benefit from using QuicShop

Stores gain valuable insight into their shoppers' preferences while they are in the store. This gives the store a unique and more focused perspective on how to maximize profit.

Behavioral Data

Gain insight into how shoppers interact with inventory.
