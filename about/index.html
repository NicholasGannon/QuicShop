The Origin

Learn the Backstory

The twin brothers Sean and Justin Regis first thought of the idea while shopping for school supplies at Costco. The store's warehouse layout made it impossible to find items so they thought of an app that helps you navigate through the store in the most efficient manner possible. After winning and gaining recognition at Union College's inaugural Sparklab competition, QuicShop successfully tested at a small store chain in Ridgewood, NJ. Now, they look to test with live shoppers at Price Chopper's Market 32 in Clifton Park, NY.
