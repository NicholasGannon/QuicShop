Beginning Stage

Starting July 2023

In July 2023, QuicShop embarked on a pioneering initiative aimed at enhancing the shopper experience at Green Way Markets in Ridgewood, New Jersey.

The study involved the formation of two distinct groups, both of whom were unfamiliar with the store's layout. These groups were entrusted with the task of locating 15 items from various sections of the store.
