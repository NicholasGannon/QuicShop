About QuicShop

How it started, and what services we provide

The Start

While shopping at Costco before the start of their Freshman year of college, Sean and Justin suddenly came across a problem. They realized how frustrating and time-consuming searching for unknown items in a large store was.

They questioned, "What if there was an app that can show you where each item is", and can take you to each item in one efficient route.

What QuicShop offers

Whether you are a regular customer roaming around a store, or a gig worker (Instacart of Shipt personal shopper), we understand how valuable your time is and how an inefficient shopping experience interferes with that.

QuicShop helps you to stop wasting time, so you can then focus on what really matters to you!
