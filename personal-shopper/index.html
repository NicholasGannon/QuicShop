What does it mean to be a Personal Shopper?

Personal Shoppers can achieve more in less time by eliminating time constraints! Learn more about how it works, in addition to the multiple benefits associated with being a Personal Shopper!

How it works

Guide on using QuicShop as a Personal Shopper

Learn the steps involved when using QuicShop as a Personal Shopper: Start by getting your list together, Navigate the selected store, and once done - Complete your trip!

Get your list

After signing up, select the store you would like to shop at, and create your unique list of items!

Navigate the store

Once your inside the store, utilize QuicShop to seamlessly navigate to each of your items and see the deals available!

Complete the trip

Once your all done finding your items, select "Complete Trip", and QuicShop will record your time spent!
