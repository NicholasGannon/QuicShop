How does QuicShop help the Shopper?

QuicShop streamlines your shopping trip while also helping you save money. Our end goal is to improve your experience & satisfaction while in the grocery store.

Learn More
How it works

Guide on using QuicShop

QuicShop is easy to use: Just make your list, find your items using navigation, and when you're done, pay on our platform. You'll be in-and-out of the store in no-time while also being shown the best deals, aisle by aisle.

Create Your Shopping List

Easily create and organize your grocery list in the app before heading to the store, ensuring you're fully prepared for a streamlined shopping experience.

Effortless Navigation & Deal Discovery

Let our smart navigation guide you to the exact locations of your items and highlight personalized deals along the way, saving you time & money as you move through the aisles.

Skip the line with One-Click Pay

After adding your last item to the cart, simply tap "Pay" to complete your purchase instantly within the app. With your card already linked, you can bypass the checkout line & enjoy a seamless, secure payment experience.
