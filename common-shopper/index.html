How does QuicShop help the Shopper?

QuicShop streamlines your shopping trip while also helping you save money. Our end goal is to improve your experience and satisfaction while in the grocery store.

How it works

Guide on using QuicShop

QuicShop is easy to use: Just make your list, find your items using navigation, and when you're done, pay on our platform. You'll be in-and-out of the store in no-time while also being shwon the best deals, aisle by aisle.

Make your list

Plan ahead and have your list ready to go before you reach the store.

Navigate to Items & Deals

Use our navigation to optimize your shopping trip, saving substantial time and money by navigating your list and the best deals within the aisles.

One-Click Pay

After finding and scanning your last item, link your credit card with our payment provider and pay for your cart on our app. Skip the checkout line!
